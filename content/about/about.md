# Zane Landers

**Astronomer • Science Writer • Digital Marketing & Communications Specialist — Tucson, Arizona**

I'm Zane Landers. I've spent the last decade turning a hands-on obsession with telescopes into a career that spans science writing, digital marketing, and public astronomy outreach. My telescope reviews and guides reach hundreds of thousands of readers through [TelescopicWatch](https://telescopicwatch.com) and Live Science, my astronomy work has been featured in TIME, National Geographic, and The Guardian, and my homemade 14.7" telescope is the [top post of all time on Reddit's r/space](https://www.reddit.com/r/space/comments/frdd1d/im_17_years_old_and_just_finished_building_this/).

I hold a Master's degree in Marketing from the University of Arizona and serve on the board of [DarkSky Southern Arizona](https://darkskysoaz.org/).

## Book a Private Star Party in Tucson

I offer **private stargazing events across Tucson and Southern Arizona** — birthdays, corporate events, weddings, family gatherings, or just a night under the darkest skies in the country. I bring excellent telescopes and a PVS-14 night vision device, and every session is a guided, screen-free tour of the Moon, planets, star clusters, galaxies, and nebulae.

I also host public stargazing evenings at the [Mount Lemmon Lodge](https://mountlemmonlodge.com/) in the Santa Catalina Mountains.
